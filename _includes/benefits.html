The Benefits of I/O Extended Las Vegas

  • Live streaming

    The Google I/O keynote and sessions will be live streaming the entire time. Listen to technical talks and hear more about Google's latest developer products.

  • Strengthen ties to the developer community

    I/O Extended events are focused on bringing the local developer community together to hear about the latest Google developments, instruct others through code labs, and share thoughts on any topic.

    The event will be held near Downtown Las Vegas within walking distance to a variety of restaurants, bars, and entertainment venues including the Fremont Street Experience and Container Park.

  • Raffle

    At least one lucky attendee will walk away with some free Google swag or possibly something better.

  • A great value

    The event is being held free of charge. Watch the I/O livestream, learn from fellow Google enthusiasts, and take part in our raffle free of charge.
